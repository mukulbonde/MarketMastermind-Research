Market Drivers and consumer preferences
Consumers are becoming more health conscious and looking for products with lower glycemic indexes and fewer calories. As a result, fruit-based sweeteners such as stevia, monk fruit, and date syrup are gaining ground. These natural sweeteners not only provide the desired sweetness but also offer additional health benefits such as antioxidants and vitamins.
