Recent developments in extraction technology are improving the efficiency and performance of fruit pulp extractors. Notable innovations include:

Smart extractors: Integration of IoT technology for real-time monitoring and optimization of the extraction process.
Energy efficient models: Reduction of energy consumption while maintaining high extraction efficiency.
