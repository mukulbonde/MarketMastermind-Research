Why freeze-dry?
The Freeze drying is a preservation method that preserves the nutritional value and flavor of foods by removing moisture at low temperatures. This process offers several advantages for pet snacks:

Nutrient preservation: Essential vitamins and minerals remain intact.
Long shelf life: Products can last for months without refrigeration.
Improved Patability: Many pets prefer the taste of freeze-dried foods.
