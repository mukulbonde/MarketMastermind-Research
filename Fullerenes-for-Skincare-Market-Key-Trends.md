What are fullerenes?
Fullerenes are spherical molecules composed of carbon atoms, resembling tiny soccer balls. They are recognized for their ability to eliminate free radicals, which can cause premature aging. Recent studies have shown that these molecules can be up to 172 times more effective than traditional antioxidants such as vitamin C.
