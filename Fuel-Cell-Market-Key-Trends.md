Market applications
Fuel cells are finding diverse applications in several sectors:

Transportation: Fuel cells are increasingly used in commercial vehicles, with companies such as Hyundai and Toyota leading the way. The introduction of hydrogen fuel cell buses and trucks is a game-changer in reducing urban emissions.
Stationary power generation: Businesses and utilities are turning to to fuel cells for reliable backup power and as a primary energy source, benefiting from their high efficiency and low emissions.
Portable power:The demand for fuel cells laptops is increasing, especially for applications in consumer electronics and military uses.
