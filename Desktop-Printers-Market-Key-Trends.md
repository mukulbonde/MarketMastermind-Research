The desktop printer market is undergoing significant transformations, determined by technological advances, consumer preferences and efforts of sustainability. In 2024, the focus will be on efficiency, environmental friendliness and easy-to-use features that suit both home and office environments.

Technological innovations driving change
One of the most notable One of the most important trends is the rise of hybrid inkjet and laser printers. These devices combine the speed of laser technology with the versatility of inkjet printing, meeting various printing needs. A recent study noted that hybrid models have seen a 15% increase in market share over the past year.

Sustainability initiatives gaining ground
With environmental concerns at the forefront, manufacturers are increasingly prioritizing sustainability. Brands such as HP and Epson have introduced programs aimed at reducing waste, such as cartridge recycling initiatives and the development of printers that use recycled materials. In fact, more than 60% of consumers now consider environmental friendliness a key factor in their purchasing decisions.

Smart features that improve the user experience
Smart technology is also leaving its mark. Features like mobile printing, voice-activated commands, and cloud connectivity are becoming standard. A survey conducted on platforms such as Reddit highlighted that 70% of users prefer printers that offer mobile compatibility, making it easy to print from anywhere.
